Founded in 2024, Ramoe is a self-graphic design project by Alya Putri Rahmasari and Yudith Pratama Ibrahim, initially driven by shared passions and values related to design, music, culture, history, and architecture. Our collective consists of passionate individuals sharing space to unleash each of our full potential. Together we built a place for the hive mind to discover and collaborate.

From these myriad passions and values, we were inspired to gather them into a body of graphic design work that represents our ideas, personalities, and expressions. We believe that collaborating together can take us beyond our limitations. Hence, it's encapsulated in the word 'ramoe' which means to gather in Bahasa.
